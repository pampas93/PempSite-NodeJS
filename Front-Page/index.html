Imagine you have a Data set in JSON format,
And wondering how or which graph to plot?
No worry, We do that for you. Just give us your Data set.

Features of Plotify

This is a web application focusing on Data Visualizing JSON Data.


  • JSON Validation
  • Graph From Dataset
  • Graph Collection

JSON Validation

Before we do any Analysing or calculations, We expect a Valid Json. So, Why not check your data right here. Almost the same functionality as jsonLint but it's right here.
Go on, Check your JSON Data, before you we can plot your graph.

Give us Data, TakeAway your Graph

Once you submit your valid JSON dataset, we do a few magic tricks, and provide you with possible graphs which support your data. All Graphs we use are supported by D3.js . A Huge shoutout to D3.
On click of your graph, we plot it for your dataset and Kaboom, you have your visualization.

Our Graph Collection

We give you options after analyzing the data set from our Graph Collection. Each Graph here, will give an insight of the look, accepted data, and a few data restrictions.
Please stay tuned as we increase our graph support database everyday, every minute.
